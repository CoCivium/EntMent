# EntMent EntMent connects entrepreneurs (Ents) with experienced advisors (Ments) through localized advisory boards and related group surfaces. ## Core idea People can build and guide in different moments. EntMent is not just a school or a consultancy. It is a mentor-founder learning and coordination surface. ## Current pillars - Advisory Boards - Trust and verification - NDA + escrow + agreements - Chat and scheduling - Resource library - Group discovery and regional coordination
