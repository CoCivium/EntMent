# Advisory Boards Advisory Boards are the core trust and mentoring unit in EntMent. ## How boards are formed - Boards are created by Ments. - Existing Ments vote on admitting additional Ments. - Ents apply to boards but are clients of the board, not members of the board itself. - Boards may be local, industry-specific, or both. ## What boards publish - specialties - mentor profiles - rates and pro-bono options - ways to request help - visible trust signals over time ## Why boards matter Boards make mentorship durable, visible, and governable. They reduce founder isolation and create a more stable alternative to one-off consulting.
